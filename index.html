A Touch of Power Series

Change. Everything changes in life, but some changes are bigger than others. Like when the video games you love become your new reality? Yeah, that’s a pretty big change. Especially when life as you know it is over, and you have to adapt just to survive.

What if a hospital bed was all you’ve ever known, and you woke up in a game world where you have to play catch up? Jade wakes up in a new place, not knowing how she got there, but realizing she can finally live. Follow her life as she figures out how to take baby steps into the real world, a world filled with magic and wonder...and monsters. She’ll have to find her own place and direction when the powers that be seem to be pulling her in a path she never wanted.
